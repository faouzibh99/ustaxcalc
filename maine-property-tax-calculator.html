About Maine Property Tax

Homestead exemption reduces assessed value by $25,000 for primary residence.

  • Average effective rate: 1.01%
  • Payment dates: October 15
  • Cumberland County rate: 1.08%
  • Homestead exemption: $25,000 off assessed value

How Maine Property Tax is Calculated

Your Maine property tax bill = Assessed Value × Tax Rate.

If you qualify for the homestead exemption, your assessed value is reduced by $25,000 before the rate is applied, saving you up to $252/year.

Rates vary by county and school district. Cumberland County has an effective rate of 1.08%, while the statewide average is 1.01%.

Maine Property Tax — FAQ

The average effective property tax rate in Maine is 1.01%. Cumberland County has a rate of 1.08%. Rates vary by county, city, and school district.
In Maine, property taxes are typically due: October 15. Check with your local county assessor for the exact deadlines in your area, as dates can vary by municipality.
