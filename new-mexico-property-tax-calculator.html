About New Mexico Property Tax

Head of family exemption of $2,000 off assessed value. Low effective rate relative to home values.

  • Average effective rate: 0.55%
  • Payment dates: November 10
  • Bernalillo County rate: 0.62%
  • Homestead exemption: $2,000 off assessed value

How New Mexico Property Tax is Calculated

Your New Mexico property tax bill = Assessed Value × Tax Rate.

If you qualify for the homestead exemption, your assessed value is reduced by $2,000 before the rate is applied, saving you up to $11/year.

Rates vary by county and school district. Bernalillo County has an effective rate of 0.62%, while the statewide average is 0.55%.
