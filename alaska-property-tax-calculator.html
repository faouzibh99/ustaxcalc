About Alaska Property Tax

No state property tax — taxes are set by boroughs and municipalities only. Senior exemption up to $150,000 off assessed value.

  • Average effective rate: 1.04%
  • Payment dates: Varies by borough
  • Anchorage rate: 1.16%
  • Homestead exemption: $150,000 off assessed value

How Alaska Property Tax is Calculated

Your Alaska property tax bill = Assessed Value × Tax Rate.

If you qualify for the homestead exemption, your assessed value is reduced by $150,000 before the rate is applied, saving you up to $1,560/year.

Rates vary by county and school district. Anchorage has an effective rate of 1.16%, while the statewide average is 1.04%.
