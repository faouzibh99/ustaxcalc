About California Property Tax

Proposition 13 caps annual increases at 2%. Homestead exemption up to $300,000 for qualifying seniors.

  • Average effective rate: 0.71%
  • Payment dates: November 1 and February 1
  • Los Angeles County rate: 0.72%
  • Homestead exemption: $300,000 off assessed value

How California Property Tax is Calculated

Your California property tax bill = Assessed Value × Tax Rate.

If you qualify for the homestead exemption, your assessed value is reduced by $300,000 before the rate is applied, saving you up to $2,130/year.

Rates vary by county and school district. Los Angeles County has an effective rate of 0.72%, while the statewide average is 0.71%.

California Property Tax — FAQ

In California, property taxes are typically due: November 1 and February 1. Check with your local county assessor for the exact deadlines in your area, as dates can vary by municipality.
