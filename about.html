About USTaxCalc

USTaxCalc was built to answer one of the most Googled financial questions in America: "How much is my property tax?" — with a real answer, not a vague estimate.

Why We Built This

Property tax is one of the largest annual expenses for American homeowners — yet most people have no idea how to calculate it accurately. The rules vary by state, county, and even city. Homestead exemptions, senior freezes, assessment ratios — it's genuinely complex.

We built USTaxCalc to simplify this. One tool, all 50 states, real 2026 data, and instant results — free forever.

Our Data

All tax rates are sourced from state tax authorities, county assessor offices, and the Tax Foundation's 2026 data. We update rates annually to reflect the latest legislation.

100% Free

USTaxCalc is and will always be free. No account needed, no email required, no paywalls. We are supported by non-intrusive display advertising.

Disclaimer

USTaxCalc provides estimates for informational purposes only. For your exact tax bill, contact your local county assessor's office. Tax laws change — always verify with official sources.
