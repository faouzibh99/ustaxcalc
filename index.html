Frequently Asked Questions

Property tax = (Home Value x Assessment Ratio) x Tax Rate. Each state sets its own assessment ratio and tax rate. Our calculators use the actual 2026 rates for your specific state and apply any homestead exemptions automatically.
A homestead exemption reduces the taxable value of your primary residence, lowering your tax bill. For example, Texas offers a $100,000 homestead exemption — meaning a $300,000 home is taxed as if it were worth $200,000.
New Jersey has the highest effective property tax rate at 2.23%, followed by Illinois (2.07%) and Connecticut (1.79%). Hawaii has the lowest rate at just 0.26%.
