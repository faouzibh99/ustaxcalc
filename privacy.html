Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026. USTaxCalc is committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What Data We Collect

USTaxCalc does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. All calculations run entirely in your browser — no data is sent to our servers.

Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This collects anonymous usage data (pages visited, time spent). No personally identifiable information is collected. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google AdSense

We display ads via Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing history. Manage your preferences at adssettings.google.com.

No Data Storage

The values you enter into our calculators are never stored or transmitted. They exist only in your browser's memory during your session.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at: privacy@ustaxcalc.app
